This year we are starting a new series of events. Each weekend we will be hosting a geek/coding/pizza-eating night in the Undergraduate Social Area in the Computer Science Department. The idea is that we can group up and do coding projects/hackathons in a chilled environment while eating more pizza than is good for us. So come down and relax whether you would like to join in in projects or just chat.

You can join the facebook group for the weekly events here: https://www.facebook.com/groups/oxcompsocgeeknight/

We also have a github page for CompSoc Members here: https://github.com/oxcompsoc

Don't forget to bring your laptop to each event. Details of dates and times for each event can be found on the termcard pages.
