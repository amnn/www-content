Annual General Meeting

Wednesday 10th March, 17:00
Lamb and Flag

The Annual General Meeting will include all the usual termly admin - motions of thanks, reports from the committee et al., and also the election of the 2010-11 committee. If you think you might be interrested in running, please contact committee@ox.compsoc.net .
