Beyond 3G technologies

Thursday 23rd February, 20:30
Denis Sciama Lecture Theatre, Denys Wilkinson Building (on Banbury Rd & Keble Rd)

John Beer, an ICT Expert to the European Commission for grand funding and evaluator for Venture Capital organisations as well as the editor of "The View", will talk on Future ICT technologies, specifically the future impacts on mobile operators and handset manufacturers design.
