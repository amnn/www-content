The video games industry - design, development, future

Speaker: Patrick O'Luanaigh

Thursday 18th May, 20:30
ComLab

Author of Game Design Complete and former head of external development at Codemasters, Patrick O'Luanaigh is now Design Director at SCi/Eidos. He'll be speaking to us about the video games industry; in particular, game design, how new games are created (such as the latest Tomb Raider installment) and some possible future trends in the industry. He'll also touch upon the best ways to get into the industry as a graduate.
