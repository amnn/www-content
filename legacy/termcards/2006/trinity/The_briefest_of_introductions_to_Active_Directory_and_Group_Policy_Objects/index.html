The briefest of introductions to Active Directory and Group Policy Objects

Speaker: James Holwell

Thursday 4th May, 20:00
(venue to be announced)

Active Directory is often given as an example of a Windows tool effectively unmatched in the Unix world in terms of comprehensiveness and ease of use. James Holwell , a maths undergrad who moonlights as a part-time sysadmin for a small business, will give us a swift tour of what it's capable of.
