Lightning Talks

Wednesday 14th May, 20:00
Keble

After the success of Michaelmas term's Lightning Talks , we're back with the same format: one room, one projector, and many speakers, each doing a short slot.

Anyone is welcome to give a talk, or just have a good laugh watching the rest of us screw up. If you'd be interested in giving a talk, contact the committee - it doesn't necessarily need to be computer-related, just interesting! We'll have a laptop and projector available, but you're welcome to bring your own laptop if you prefer.

There'll be someone at Keble lodge to take you to the room.
