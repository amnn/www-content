NaturalMotion Euphoria in the Real World

Wednesday 25th January, 18:00
St Catz JCR Lecture Theatre

"Euphoria in the Real World - A short history of taking revolutionary procedural animation from academic research to commercial success." Delivered by Simon Mack, CTO of NaturalMotion.

NaturalMotion is a leading game technology and development company based in Oxford (England) and San Francisco (California). The company's award-winning euphoria engine is a break-through in real-time character animation and is used in AAA titles such as Rockstar Games' Grand Theft Auto IV, Red Dead Redemption, and LucasArts' Star Wars: The Force Unleashed.

Week 3

Google My other computer is a data center

Wednesday 1st February, 20:15
St Catz JCR Lecture Theatre

"My other computer is a data center" - The warehouse-scale computing infrastructure at Google

Christoph Best, Software Engineer, Google

Google's mission, to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful, is simple, but it entails running a large number of very different applications from web search and advertising over video, geo and commerce to enterprise. All these activities are supported by a single underlying computing infrastructure: Google's worldwide data centers.

In this talk, I will try to give an impressions how it is to work at Google directly which such a unique infrastructure, and what its implications for computer science could be, with an emphasis on large-scale data processing.
