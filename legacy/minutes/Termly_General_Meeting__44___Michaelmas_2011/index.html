Termly General Meeting, Michaelmas 2011

The meeting was held in St Catherine's College, on the 30th of November 2011. It was opened at 18:12, with the whole committee plus additional members in attendance

Apologies for absence

None received

Officers' reports

Treasurer's report

Laurens reports a rise in finances over the term, attributed to sponsorship from Deloitte, Gloucester Research, Metaswitch, Jane Street Capital and Netcraft. A sponsorship deal has been agreed with BoAML potentially for 3 years starting next year. We received more offers, but have been hesitant to accept them so that we can focus on what we have, and to not have too much money. BoAML and Netcraft events were the most popular in this term. We have enough money to not need to charge fees, but will continue to do so for caution, and also to let people feel they are invested in the society. We're looking to get membership cards printed next term. Ben proposes approving the accounts, seconded by Ian. Unanimously passes.

Secretary's report

President's report

CompSoc has done well this term, with the biggest yearly growth the society has ever seen. This is attributed to having more money, so we can afford to have smaller fees and more pizza. Although this term has gone well we need to look forward and prepare for next term, and start thinking about the next committee so that the society doesn't run out of steam. We plan to start advertising for the new committee in an email at the beginning of next term.

'Univeristy' title

We hope to become Oxford University Computer Society next term, as we will have been registered for the required two terms by then.
