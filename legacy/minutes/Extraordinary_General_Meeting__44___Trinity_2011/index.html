Extraordinary General Meeting, Trinity 2011

The meeting was held in St Catherine's College on 10th May 2011. It was opened at 18:10, with the following members in attendance:

  • Dominic Hargreaves (DH), Senior Member
  • Ben Gazzard
  • Ian Jones
  • Laurens Lemaire
  • Alex Kavvos
  • Michael Howe (MH)
  • David North (DN)
  • Luke Cartey
  • Valentin Aslanyan (VA)

Apologies for absence

Stephen White and Huw Pryce sent apologies.

Senior member's report

DH reported briefly on the hiatus which had arisen in the society last year, owing to the lack of a committee. This included a report on the finances on the committee, which had included some transactions during the year to keep society infrastructure alive, and expenses to cover the necessary disposal of some server hardware.

Elections

There were four candidates for committee. They had been proposed as follows:

  • Ben Gazzard was nominated as President by VA and seconded by MH
  • Alex Kavvos was nominated as Vice-President by VA and seconded by MH
  • Ian Jones was nominated as Secretary by DN and seconded by VA
  • Laurens Lemaire was nominated as Treasurer by DN and seconded by VA

The four candidates were accepted unanimously.

Any other business

Membership fees

DH proposed that, owing to the society hiatus, the membership fees for the members joining that evening be reduced to the freshers rate as posted on the society website.

The motion was accepted.

Any other business

There was none.

The meeting was closed at 18:25.
