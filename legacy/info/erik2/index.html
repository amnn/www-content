Erik's sabbatical

Compsoc regional headquarters, 1:52pm, 8/7/03

In response to a heartfelt appeal by OUSGG members, Erik has decided to cut his sabbatical short and return to them.

Erik said:

"I hope that this show of good faith will help relations between the two societies".

We remain hopeful that OUSGG will respond in kind by repealing the offending motion at the next opportunity.
